11.10.2009 MIBC Spotlight: Mother and daughter team up for Home United

by Chen Hui Jing

The Home United Basketball Club from Singapore is undoubtedly the team with the most interesting mix of players, from various nationalities as well as age-groups.

What most people do not know is that Xiu Lijuan, wearing the jersey number 17, is no ordinary 50-year-old. She is a former-member of the China National Women's Basketball team, who helped team China win the bronze medal in the 1983 World Basketball Championship for Women. She is also an Olympic-medalist, playing in the 1984 Olympics held in Los Angeles, USA. And most importantly, she is the mother of Home United stand-out, Yao Xiuxiu.

The original plan was simply to pay a visit to Xiuxiu, who is currently studying in Singapore. However, as the Home United team was under-manned for this tournament, Coach Tan Yee Nee jokingly suggested that she suit up for them as well. Both mother and daughter felt this was a wonderful idea and here they are now, playing on the same court for this edition of the Malaysia International Basketball Championship.

When asked how she felt about playing with her daughter, Xiu Li juan said, "I'm really happy to see that my daughter plays with a lot of heart. To see her perform well on court gives me a peace of mind, that all her sacrifices were not in vain. My only qualm is that she shies away from body contact and is not tough enough yet. I hope that she will continue to develop and improve on her skills in the years to come."

"I was looking forward to this competition as I had never got the chance to play on the same team as my mum before. There wasn't much pressure or anything before the game either. I'm really proud to see my mother still being able to play and score, I think she still has it in her," said Xiuxiu, when asked about how she felt playing with her mother.

It is not often we get to see a mother-daughter combination on the basketball court, but evidently, both Xiu Lijuan and her daughter, Yao Xiuxiu, share a special bond both on and off the court.

母女党并肩作战

内政群英可说是本赛会最具有特色的球队了，她们的球员都来自不同国家及年龄层。 不说你不知，身穿十七号球衣的修丽娟，来头可不小哦。

现年五十岁的她，是前中国女篮副队长。在国家队，曾获第九届亚运会女子篮球比赛冠军，１９８３年第九届世界女子篮球锦标赛第三名，１９８４年第二十三届奥运会女子篮球比赛第三名。这位前奥运选手也是内政群英主力球员，姚修修的母亲。

原本她只是想前去新加坡探望在那儿念书的姚修修。但是，由于内政群英队正为了次球赛人手不足的问题烦恼，陈教练便以开玩笑的方式建议 “姚妈“披上战袍助阵。 两母女都认为这确实是个不错的建议，所以她们因此获得了母女一起上同场竞技的难得机会。

母女俩也从未有过一起上场比赛的经验。”第一次跟女儿同场竞技。看到她打球很拼，有所表现，还感到蛮开心。因为这证明了她的复出不是白费的。只觉得她还打得不够硬朗，胆子较小。希望她在篮球方面会再接再厉，继续加油。“

与母亲共同参赛，修修也有所感言，“对于这次的比赛，我还算是满期待的，因为长这么大，第一次和妈妈一起上场。比赛时也没什么压力。每当有成功的配合进球时就特别开心。看到妈妈还宝刀未老，真的感到很骄傲。“
