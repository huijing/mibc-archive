09.10.2009 Hong Kong China defeat Zhuhai Enterprise Group 97-76

by Chen Hui Jing

The Hong Kong China team picked themselves up from last night's loss to the hosts and beat the older Zhuhai Enterprise team 97-76.

The Hong Kong China team led from start to finish and after 3 days of play, signs of fatigue were showing from the Zhuhai squad. Hong Kong controlled the tempo of the game, forcing the Chinese to run with them. Hong Kong had a 10-point lead going into the second half with score at 46-36.

Chinese center, Lin Jin, hit 2 free-throws at the 4:10 mark of the third quarter to cut the Hong Kong lead to four, but that was as close as the Chinese would get. The Hong Kong team started to pull away during the fourth quarter.

Tempers started to flare after a slew of fouls called on the Chinese half-way through the fourth quarter. Zhang Ji Xing got called for two consecutive technical fouls and sat out the rest of the game. The Hong Kong team were unfazed by all this and won the game with a comfortable margin of 21 points.

Hong Kong China had five players scoring in double-figures and shot 51% from the field. Fong Shing Yee had 16 points and 7 rebounds, and Poon Chi Ho contributed 15 points and 6 assists. Lo Yi Ting, Lau Chi Kin and Li Kim Wong finished with 14, 12 and 11 points respectively.

From the Zhuhai team, Li Jin led all scorers with 19 points and also grabbed 7 rebounds. Sun Zhong Cheng finished with a double-double of 12 points and 10 rebounds.

 

 

 

（吉隆坡9日訊）第2屆馬來西亞國際籃球邀請賽今日進入第3天爭奪，香港火力全開轟下超過五成命中率，終場以97比76痛擊中國珠海免稅集團，戰績提升為2胜1負。　

港隊似乎沒有受到昨晚不敵東道主大馬男籃的影響，特別是進攻套的執教方面相當成功，上、下半場的團隊命中率均維持水準，令珠海徒嘆奈何。　此役港隊全場73投37中，命中率高達51％，三分球也有44％的出色表現（27投12中），轟得珠海七暈八素。另外，盡管港隊身高處劣勢，但他們在保守籃板球方面做得不錯，竟以31比29稍占優勢。

　劉子健和李劍煌是港隊上半場火力最猛的兩名球員，兩人分別攻下12及11分，加上羅意庭在半場結束前的壓哨三分球，幫助港隊建立46比36的雙位數優勢。　珠海第3節由張輝和李晉帶頭追分，曾將差距縮小至52比56，但港隊火力再度爆發打出一輪15比3的攻擊波，瞬間將比分擴大為71比55，末節終段一度將優勢擴大為22分，珠海宣告無力回天。

個人表現方面，港隊以方城義的16分7籃板為最佳，潘志豪15分5籃板6助攻，羅意庭三分球5投4中轟遭下14分；李晉為珠海攻下全場最高的19分7籃板，曲紹華和劉海龍各得16分，孫忠誠12分10籃板。
