09.10.2009 Chosun University notch their first win


by Chen Hui Jing

The Chosun University boys managed to eke out their first victory after a physical game against the Philippines Pharex Bidang Generix team. The pace of the game was fast and furious, with both teams pushing the pace with a running game. The Koreans prevailed in the end and won with a score of 92-81.

Four Korean players scored in double-figures, with Joung Jin Gon leading all scorers with 30 points. Lee Dae Yeon scored 12 points and both Baek Ho Seung and Kim Da Woon scored 10 apiece.

The Filipino side also had four players with double-digit scoring, Alfredo Gerilla Jr had 24 points and 5 rebounds, Conrad Fritz Bauzon scored 16 points while John Carl Melegrito and James Mangahas contributed 13 points and 12 points respectively.

 

兩支此前2戰全敗的球隊正面交鋒，雙方在這場近身肉搏戰中擦出零星火花，所幸火勢沒有變大，最終韓國朝鮮大學以92比81力挫菲律賓次級聯賽PBL亞軍法樂仕。　

首嚐胜果的韓隊目前戰績為1胜2負，法樂仕無奈吞下3連敗。鄭晉坤左沖右突飆進全場最高的32分，李大淵12分，白承昊和金斗元各得10分；格里亞為法樂仕攻進22分，鮑松16分，梅萊格里托13分，曼加哈斯12分。
