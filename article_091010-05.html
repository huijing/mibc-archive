10.10.2009 Our boys come back from behind to win

by Chen Hui Jing

It was a good night for the Malaysian teams as both the men and women came back from behind to win their respective games. Our boys roared back from a deficit that was a large as 16 at one point to clinch their third win of the tournament, beating the Chosun University team 97-83.

The Koreans opened the game with a 9-2 run, as our boys had yet to find their shooting touch. The Chosun University boys shot the ball well hitting four 3-pointers in the first quarter. The Malaysian team responded with inside scoring, making most of their baskets in the paint. The Koreans led by 10 at the end of the first quarter.

The Chosun University boys continued to light it up from three-point range and Kim Da Woon hit a shot to give the Koreans their biggest lead of the night at 16. The Malaysians buckled down and tightened their defense to prevent the lead from ballooning any further.

Loh Shee Fai came into the game with 5:36 remaining in the second quarter and scored 7 points off the bench. Ho Yeoh Loong drained a three-pointer with 27 seconds left on the clock to cut the Korean lead to 5.

Shots started to fall for the Malaysians and a 3-pointer by John Ng tied the game at 68 with eight minutes left in the game. Both teams found their shooting range and traded baskets up and down the floor. A 3-pointer by Guganeswaran gave Malaysia a one-point lead with 5:45 remaining and the Malaysians never looked back. The Malaysians won the game 93-87.

Malaysia had four players scoring in double figures. Chee Li Wei led the way with 26 points making 7-of-10 from the field and hitting all 12 of his free throw attempts. Loh Shee Fai, who had won the 3-point shootout earlier this evening, carried on his hot shooting streak over to the game and hit 8-of-10 from the field for 18 points. Guganeswaran Batumalai also scored 18 points, hitting 3 of his 5 attempts from beyond the arc, and John Ng chipped in with 12 points.

The Chosun University side also had four players scoring in double figures. Joung Jin Gon scored 19 points and dished out 8 assists. Center, Kim Da Woon also contributed 18 points. Kim Hwi Min and Baek Ho Seung scored 17 points and 11 points respectively.

 

 

 

 

 
 


（吉隆坡10日訊）第2屆馬來西亞國際籃球邀請賽今日進入第4天爭奪，東道主馬來西亞頂著對手的三分炮彈后，終于在最后一節的爆發，以93比87力挫韓國朝鮮大學，重回勝利軌道。

儘量朝鮮大學內線吃虧，但他們利用精準的外線讓大馬陷入若戰，上半場韓國人在三分線就出手20次，並轟中9球，命中率達45％。

此外，穩健的團隊命中率，讓朝鮮大學在首節結束就建立10分（28比18）的領先優勢。

朝鮮大學還一度取得14分的差距，但羅樹輝和何耀隆在次節末端的爆發，聯手拉出一波12比3的攻擊波，讓大馬成功在半場結束時將比分縮小至5分（47比42）。

下半場黃有成爆發一人連得8分，此時大馬只落后4分（50比54），而大馬也改打全場盯人以阻斷對手的三分炮彈，此后大馬帶著3分劣勢進入末節。

黃有成在比賽還有7分多鐘時，轟入振起全隊士氣的三分球，並將比分扳成68平，此后雙方緊咬比分，並多次將比分追平。

最后5分鐘古加尼射進反超的關鍵三分球，讓主隊以78比77領先，此后主隊火力再度爆發打出一輪9比3的攻擊波，將比分擴大為88比80，最終朝鮮大學宣告無力回天，以6分之差落敗。
