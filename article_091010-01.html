10.10.2009 Philippines Pharex Bidang Generix shoot the lights out
by Chen Hui Jing

The Philippines Pharex Bidang Generix team came out with a lot of energy today. It was apparent that the Filipino boys were feeling good about themselves this afternoon. Shooting a fantastic 54% from 3-point range, the Pharex Bidang Generix team pulled off their first win against the Hong Kong China team 97-92.

The Philippines got off to a great start, going on a 10-2 run after the opening tip. However, the Hong Kong team warmed up soon after and went on their own 8-0 run to even the score. The Filipino side continued to shoot the ball well, with most of their shots finding the bottom of the net, the score was 28-18 at the end of the first quarter. The Pharex Bidang Generix team continued their hot shooting streak into the second quarter and had an 11 point cushion going into halftime.

Hong Kong team found their shooting touch in the second half and consecutive turnovers by the Philippines allowed the Hong Kong team to cut the lead to 4 two minutes into the third quarter. Lau Chi Kin hit all four of his 3-pointers in this quarter and cut the Filipino lead to 3 by the end of the third quarter.

With practically every Filipino player hitting their shots, there was not much the Hong Kong team could do and the Pharex Bidang Generix team had a 12 point lead with 1 minute remaining in the fourth quarter. However, the Hong Kong China team were not about to roll over and give up the win so easily. With time running out, the Hong Kong China team elected to press and foul the Filipinos to stop the clock.

The Hong Kong China team started to hit from beyond the arc. Wu Kwok Fung and Li Wai Lun combined to make five 3-pointers in the last minute. The Filipinos kept their cool though, making most of their free throw attempts to stave off the Hong Kong charge for the win.

The Philippines were 13-for-24 from three-point range and five players scoring in double-figures. Alfredo Gerilla Jr scored 20 points off 8-for-12 shooting from the field and also grabbed 10 rebounds. Conrad Fritz Bauzon, Von Harry Lanete and Ian Chris Mazo all had 15 points each, most of them coming from beyond the arc. Jerrick Canada also had 14 points.

The Hong Kong China team also had five players with double-digit scoring. Li Wai Lun, led all scorers with 23 points off 6-for-9 shooting from beyond the arc, while Lau Chi Kin scored 16 points off 4-for-9 shooting from three-point range as well. Poon Chi Ho, Kwok Fung Wu and Fong Shing Yee scored 14, 10 and 12 points respectively.

 

 
 

菲律賓次級聯賽球隊法樂仕則以97比92險勝香港，結束3連敗。

菲隊5人得分上雙，三分球24投13中。在籃板球方面，兩隊同得34個。菲隊的失誤比香港少兩次（11比13）。香港在最后1分33秒的三分轟炸險些讓菲律賓法樂仕的15分優勢化為烏有，三分球命中率高達42%（48投20中），只可惜僅獲得10次罰球機會，并罰入6球，讓在罰球線29投取走22分的菲隊奪走最后的勝利。不過港隊在保守籃板球方面做得不錯，竟以對手打成34平。

李偉倫拿下全場最高的23分，中鋒李偉倫三分球9投6中得23分、5籃板和4助攻。劉子健16分，CBA后衛潘志豪貢獻14分、8籃板、4助攻和4偷球。菲律賓有5人得分上雙，但以格里拉的火力最強，拿下20分、10籃板。
