10.10.2009 Pure Youth win four-in-a-row

by Chen Hui Jing

The Pure Youth Construction team has won their fourth consecutive game, beating the Zhuhai Enterprise Group team soundly with a score of 94-66.

The Pure Youth team were faster, fitter and more aggressive than their Chinese counterparts. Li Jin from Zhuhai opened up the score with a dunk to give the Chinese their only lead of the game at 2-0. The Pure Youth team played their usual run-and-gun style of basketball and scored most of their points off Zhuhai turnovers. The Pure Youth team led by 8 at the end of the first quarter.

With the Pure Youth team constantly pushing the pace and pressuring the Zhuhai team on defense, the Zhuhai players started to show signs of fatigue in the second quarter. However, Zhuhai was able to hit from the perimeter, preventing the Pure Youth boys from runnig away with the game. The score at half-time was 46-32 in favour of Pure Youth.

The Pure Youth team were cold from the perimeter, shooting a dismal 19% from three-point range. Still, the Taiwanese were able to sustain their double-digit lead as the Zhuhai squad were unable to defend the more athletic Pure Youth team's inside penetration and fast breaks. The Pure Youth team pulled away during the fourth quarter and finished the game with a 28 point margin.

The Pure Youth team outscored the Zhuhai Enterprise team 52-28 on points in the paint and forced the Chinese into committing 28 turnovers.

Four Pure Youth players scored in double-figures. Chang Yu-Lin had 19 points to lead the team, Hung Chi-Feng scored 15 points and grabbed 7 rebounds. Tsai Wen-Cheng and Chen Chia-Chieh chipped in with 12 points and 10 points respectively.

On the Zhuhai side, three players scored in double-figures. Zhao Ren Bin led all scorers with 24 points, while Qu Shao Hua and Liu Hai Long added 10 points apiece.

 

 

 

 

 
 


（吉隆坡10日訊）輕取中國珠海免稅集團后，中華台北璞園建設築以4連勝姿態提前鎖定冠軍。

第2屆馬來西亞國際籃球邀請賽今日進入第4天爭奪，在一場海峽兩岸打比戰中，珠海免稅集團和璞園建設築正面交鋒，雙方在這場近身肉搏戰中擦出零星火花，所幸火勢沒有變大，最終璞園建設築以94比66力挫對手，拿下4連勝。

此役結束后，璞園建設築成為本屆賽會僅存的唯一不敗隊伍，同時也提前成為本屆賽會的霸主。

值得一提，珠海免稅集團的灌籃王李晉、劉海龍和璞園建設築的鄭至豪、吳健龍均犯滿離場。

珠海免稅集團只與璞園建設築對抗了一節后，接著就被對手將比分拉開，並以32比45落后13分結束上半場。 儘管珠海免稅集團在隨后的比賽還是頑強反擊，但由于之前失血過多，加上球員體力不濟，最后以28分之差慘敗。

個人表現方面，璞園建設築以張羽霖的19分為最佳，黃啟峰15分7籃板，蔡文誠也攻下14分；趙仁斌為珠海攻下全場最高的24分7籃板，曲紹華和劉海龍各得10分，孫忠誠9分7籃板。
