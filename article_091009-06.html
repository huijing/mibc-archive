09.10.2009 Malaysia downed by Pure Youth Construction

by Chen Hui Jing

Our boys suffered their first defeat of the tournament against the Pure Youth Construction team. Coach Goh had commented the day before that our boys only had a 20% chance of victory. True to his words, the Malaysia Men's team were unable to penetrate the impregnable Taiwanese defense and the Pure Youth team avenged last year's loss with aplomb.

The boys from Pure Youth Construction had come to play tonight, putting on their strongest players to start the game for the first time since the beginning of the tournament. The Pure Youth team's hard-nosed defense neutralised our two big men, Chee Li Wei and Koh Way Tek, and our boys trailed by 8 going into the second quarter.

Guganeswaran and Loh Shee Fai managed to hit from beyond the arc but the Malaysian defense was unable to contain Tso Tsung-Kai, who drained three 3-pointers to extend the Taiwanese lead to 13, and score was 50 to 29 at half-time.

The Pure Youth team were far from finished as they went on a 14-0 run to open the second half, and ended the third quarter with a whopping 30-point lead. The boys from Taipei seemed intent on making their prowess known and did not let up at all, giving the hosts to chance to get back into the game.

James Mao, from Pure Youth, led all scorers with 17 points, while Chee Li Wei, from Malaysia, had a double-double with 16 points and 11 rebounds.

 

 

 

 

 
 

(吉隆坡9日讯）马来西亚国家男篮队未能延续胜利之旅，今天在第二届国际篮球邀请赛以93比74不敌夺冠热门中华台北璞园建设，首尝败绩。 国家队教练吴清发昨天坦言此役仅有二成胜机，结果一直未能撕破对手防线的马来西亚队，让璞园建设如愿回报了去年的5分不敌之辱。

取得三连胜的璞园建设由始至终采取紧逼防守出击马来西亚，彻底封锁高佬陈利伟和许伟德的发挥，让主队首节以18比26落后。 罗树辉和古加尼次节成功在3分线开斋，但却失守璞园建设射手左从凯，让后者一口气轰进3粒远程炮，把比分扩大至37比24，并以50比29领先上半场。

璞园建设下半场打出一波14比0攻击球，第3节结束以30分把马来西亚队远抛后头。进入末节，璞园建设依旧不松懈，叫马来西亚毫无逆转的机会。 毛加恩为璞园建设拿下最高17分；陈利伟是仅有打出二双的球员，拿下16分和11个篮板。
