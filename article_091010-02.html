10.10.2009 Guangzhou on target to win inaugural women's category

by Chen Hui Jing

The Guangzhou Youth team got the better of the Haishan High School girls, winning comfortably with a score of 88-72, and further securing their chances of winning the inaugural women's category of the MIBC.

In what seemed to be a game between two evenly matched teams, both sides took a while to get into a shooting rhythm. The smaller Haishan girls collapsed into a zone defense to deal with the Guangzhou inside threat of 1.95m, Yu Dong. The Haishan girls worked hard on defense and pushed the pace whenever they got the chance. The first quarter ended with the Haishan girls trailing by 2.

The Guangzhou girls started to pound the ball inside and the Haishan girls found it hard to deal with Yu Dong, who scored 11 points in the second quarter. The score at half-time was 37-28 in favour of the Guangzhou team.

Both teams traded baskets throughout the third quarter and the Haishan girls were unable to reduce the Guangzhou lead despite their best efforts on defense. Shots started to fall for both teams in the fourth quarter, with Yang Li Wei from Guangzhou, and Liu Jung-Hsing from Haishan both hitting three 3-pointers each.

However, the Guangzhou team's dominance inside the paint kept the Haishan girls from getting close, and with Guangzhou's hitting from the perimeter as well, the Guangzhou maintained their lead all the way till the end of the game.

Both teams played a six-player rotation, with four players from each team scoring in double figures. Both Yang Li Wei and Yu Dong from Guangzhou had 26 points apiece. Yang Xi and Qiu Si Yue scored 16 and 13 points respectively. Yu Dong also grabbed 12 rebounds for a double-double.

On the Haishan side, sharpshooter Liu Jung-Hsing, scored 21 points off 5-for-8 from three-point range. Forward, Hsu Chien-Hui had a double-double with 19 points and 14 rebounds. Liu Jia-Wei and Huang Hsian-Ting scored 14 and 12 points respectively.

 

 
 

中挫台鎖定冠軍

在一場海峽兩岸打比戰，廣州青年隊以88比72力挫中華台北海山高中，贏得2連勝，同時也為她鎖定冠軍。

這支平均年齡才16.5歲的隊伍，打得虎虎生威，全場68投34中，命中率高達50％，三分球也有41％的出色表現（17投7中）。

廣州青年隊今仗有4人得分上雙，其中以楊力維和于東的火力最猛，兩人各別攻下26分，不過后者還拉下12個籃板，連續2場比賽交出雙十成績。 海山高中同樣有4人得分上雙，劉容杏攻下全隊最高的21分，主力徐千惠也有19分，同時還有14個籃板。

目前廣州青年隊拿下2戰全勝，海山高中與大馬則1勝1負，內政群英2連敗。 由于內政群英在關鍵戰敗給大馬，因此廣州青年隊就算明日與內政群英一仗落敗也不會影響大局。
