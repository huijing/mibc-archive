10.10.2009 Malaysia prevail in overtime thriller

by Chen Hui Jing

The Malaysia Women's team managed to hold off the Singapore Home United Basketball Club in a nail-biting overtime thriller to win 73-68.

Cheryl Poon from Home United drained a 3-pointer to open the game. However, our girls went on a 9-0 run immediately after. The Malaysian defense managed to force the Home United players to turn the ball over and extended their lead to seven to close out the first quarter.

The second quarter brought more of the same as both teams traded baskets, with Zhang Shu scoring five consecutive points to cut the lead Malaysian lead to 2 at the 7:17 mark. However, a flurry of turnovers near the end of the second quarter saw the Malaysians pull away and Kew Suik May made a lay-up at just before the half-time buzzer to give Malaysia a 12-point lead with the score at 36-24.

Home United found their shooting rhythm in the second half, with Zhang Shu and Cheryl Poon each hitting from beyond the arc to cut the Malaysian lead to 6 just two minutes into the second half. As the Home United girls found their shooting form, the Malaysians seemed to lose theirs, allowing Home United to creep back into the game, ending the third quarter trailing only by 4 points.

The hosts seemed flustered going into the fourth quarter and with Home United shooters, Zhang Shu and Cheryl Poon heating up from beyond the arc, the Malaysian lead was in peril. Zhang Shu knocked down a 3-pointer with 7:24 left to give Home United their first lead since the 9:10 mark of the first quarter. Cheryl Poon hit another 3-pointer from way beyond the three-point line to extend the Home United lead to six. But Kew Suik May answered right back with her own long-range bomb, igniting the home crowd.

Home United had a six point lead with one minute remaining and Coach Tan called his final time-out of the game to settle down his players nerves. Pee Yann Yann drove the lane, scoring on a reverse lay-up and was fouled with 36 seconds left on the clock. She calmly swished the free throw, cutting the Home United lead to 3.

The Malaysians employed their trademark full court press and managed to force Home United to turn the ball over, however, Goh Beng Fong was called for a charge while driving into the lane and fouled out with 6 points. Zhao Jing hit 1-of-2 free throws to give Home United a four-point cushion with 10 seconds remaining.

Pee Yann Yann was fouled and went to the line, hitting 1-of-2 free throws. Home United may have miscalculated by calling a time-out at this point, giving Coach Tan a chance to draw up a play for sharpshooter Ang Siew Teng. She managed to get open and hit a clutch 3-pointer with 3 seconds left to tie the game at 61-61. Yao Xiuxiu threw up a prayer from half-court, which barely missed, sending the game into overtime.

The Home United team were exhausted at this point, with most of their key players already playing more than thirty minutes, and were playing entirely on heart and willpower. The Malaysians, in contrast, were raring to go, with Pee Yann Yann opening the period with a wide-open layup after blowing past her defender.

With key player Yao Xiuxiu fouling out with 3:04 left to play, Home United's chances of winning seemed bleak. But the girls from Home United never stopped fighting, and Li Xue Ling made a 3-pointer with 16 seconds left to cut the Malaysian lead to 4. Home United were unable to get any closer and Kew Suik May hit 1-of-2 free throws to clinch the match for the Malaysians with a final score of 73-68.

Three Home United players scored in double figures. Zhang Shu led the team with 20 points off 4-for-6 shooting from three-point range. Cheryl Poon had a double-double with 18 points and 15 rebounds and Yao Xiuxiu had 15 points.

The Malaysian side also saw three players scoring double-digits. Pee Yann Yann led all scorers with 24 points, making 10-for-14 from the field. Kew Suik May and Ang Siew Teng also contributed 10 points each.

 

 

 

加時氣走新加坡 馬女撿回勝利

汪秀婷在最后2.4秒的扳平，加上彭燕燕在加時獨得7分，助馬來西亞在第2屆馬來西亞國際籃球邀請賽，以73比68險勝新加坡內政群英。 大馬似乎沒有受到昨晚不敵廣州青年的影響，特別是進攻套路的執行方面相當成功。

彭燕燕和邱淑媚2人在次節聯手打出一波12比4的進攻潮，讓大馬將比分拉大至33比22，彭燕燕一人在這輪攻擊波中獨得10分。主隊以36比24結束上半場。 下半場主隊打得有些急躁，讓內政群英趁機在第3節還有1分多鐘時，將比分縮小至41比45。

末節比賽進入白熱化，內政群英利用一波10比0攻擊波將比分反超53比47，不過邱淑媚和汪秀婷的進球，讓大馬只后1分（52比54）。

不過內政群英的張姝和姚修修再祭出一波7比2的進攻波，將差距拉開至60比54、61比58，眼看大勢已去的主隊，在比賽還剩2.4秒時，汪秀婷轟進關鍵三分球，將比分扳成61平，進入加時。 加時賽，在彭燕燕獨得7分的支援下，以73比68險勝內政群英，拿下首場勝利。

中国报 蕭鉦強報導
