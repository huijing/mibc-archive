07.10.2009 香港轻松战胜朝鲜大学 方城义罗意庭表现突出

‧中国报 叶正欣报道

（吉隆坡7日訊）第2屆馬來西亞國際籃球邀請賽今日開打，在李劍煌替補出場攻下17分的帶動下，香港首戰以81比68擊敗韓國朝鮮大學，輕松打響首炮。 　

除了李劍煌之外，禁區大將方城義添上11分7籃板，控衛羅意庭則有10分4籃板4助攻的表現。港隊此役命中率達到46％，優于對手的38％，禁區得分也以48比22占據壓倒性優勢。

朝鮮大學也有3人得分上雙，白承昊三分球10投6中射下全場最高的25分，中鋒李大淵搶下15分，金斗元有13分8籃板進帳。從首仗情況來看，朝鮮大學有韓國人机動性打法的影子，可惜實力有限。

這是香港連續第2年受邀參賽，去年在主力沒來齊的情況下，最終5戰全敗扛大旗，但這次為了備戰年底的東亞運動會，香港派出了強陣出擊。

朝鮮大學今年取代慶熙大學首次參賽，整體實力一直是個謎，甚至連經常帶隊到韓國打比賽的璞園主帥許晉哲，也對這支球隊一無所知。

港隊一開場嚐試用潘志豪和羅意庭兩名控衛搭檔后場，但似乎沒能取得預期中的效果，朝鮮大學在首節還2分43秒時一度以21比11領先10分。

但港隊隨后之加強防守並調入得分爆炸力極強的鄭錦興，后者一上場便連取4分，幫助港隊打出一波跨節的21比2攻勢，次節末段以32比23反超，半場結束時建立42比34的優勢。

港隊第3節“打蛇隨棍上”，曾將比分拉遠至57比41領先多達16分，並以63比54進入第4節。朝鮮大學試圖反扑，奈何個人技術有所欠缺，很難突破港隊嚴密的盯人防守，始終無法縮小差距至8分之內。

 

 

 

 


The 2nd Malaysia International Basketball Championships tipped off earlier today. With Li Kim Wong's 17 points off the bench, the Hong Kong China team managed to overpower the boys from Korea Chosun University with a scoreline of 81 to 68. Big man, Fong Shing Yee, contributed 11 points and 7 rebounds,and point guard, Lo Yi Ting, also chipped in with 10 points and 4 assists.

The Hong Kong team had a shooting percentage of 46% while their Korean opponents only managed 38% from the field. The Hong Kong team also had a much stronger inside game, with 48 of their 81 points coming from within the paint.

The Chosun University team had 3 players scoring in double digits, with Baek Seung Ho lighting it up from beyond the arc, hitting six of his ten 3-point attempts and scoring a total of 25 points. Center, Lee Dae Yeon, scored 15 points and Kim Dong Woo contributed 13 points and 8 rebounds.

The Chosun University boys played a quick and aggressive game, which is traditionally the Korean style of play, however, they were unable to out-run the Hong Kong team. This is Hong Kong China's second time participating in the Malaysian International Basketball Championship and this time around they brought their best players to fight for the title.

Last year's Hong Kong China team may have finished last, but they were without their starters. This time around, however, the Hong Kong China team has sent its strongest squad in preparation for the East Asian games which will be held at the end of the year. The Chosun University team replaced the Kyung Hee University team as the Korean representatives and this is their first time participating in the tournament.

The Hong Kong team started off the game with two point guards, Poon Chi Ho and Lo Yi Ting, but were unable to control the rhythm of the game and ended up 10 points behind Chosun University with 2 minutes and 43 seconds remaining in the first quarter. The Hong Kong team tightened up their defense and Cheng Kam Hing scored 4 points off the bench, sparking off a 21 to 2 run thereafter. The score at half time was 42 to 34 in favour of the Hong Kong team.

The Hong Kong team continued to ride high during the second half, pushing their lead to 16 at one point in the game. The Chosun University boys tried their best to turn the tide but were unable to penetrate the man-to-man defense played by the Hong Kong team and were unable to cut the lead to less than 8 points.
