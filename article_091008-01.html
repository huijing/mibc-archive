08.10.2009 刘海龙得24分14篮板 珠海队法乐仕尝首胜

‧中国报 叶正欣报道

（吉隆坡8日訊）鋒將劉海龍個人獨取24分14籃板，率領中國珠海免稅集團在第2屆馬來西亞國際籃球邀請賽次仗，知恥而后勇以73比66力擒菲律賓次級聯賽亞軍法樂仕，拿下首胜。 　

經歷昨晚的慘敗后，珠海重整戰力並作出調整，較年輕的控衛張輝和大前鋒李晉獲得首發机會，取代張繼興和趙仁斌，球隊的攻防速度明顯有所提升，與首仗完全判若兩隊。

此役劉海龍一開場即進入狀態，首節就已砍進了10分，助球隊以19比11取得領先，次節剩3分57秒再次三分中鵠，一度將領先優勢擴大為雙位數（31比21），並以36比29的差距進入下半場。

法樂仕在第3節展開反扑，並在此節還剩8分多鐘時追至38比39僅落后1分。珠海隨后打出一波13比4的攻勢，差距重新拉大為10分，但拉內特接著連中兩記三分球，3節打完比分又變為48比54。

末節法樂仕仍在苦苦追趕，甚至不惜在最后5分鐘試圖采取全場緊逼收復失地，但也付出了得分主力馬索5 犯離場的代價，終場前3分09秒曲紹華完成一次“3分打”后，珠海以69比59奠下胜基。

法樂仕之前就因為“灌籃王”埃斯皮里圖征戰國內大學聯賽而缺陣，主控梅萊格里托又在首戰嘴部受傷高掛免戰牌，結果只剩9人應戰的法樂仕雖已拚盡全力，但最終仍無法改變2連敗的厄運。

此役珠海團隊命中率有所回升達到44％，除了劉海龍之外，孫忠誠貢獻14分9籃板，曲紹華交出11分11籃板的雙十成績，張輝也有10分7籃板3助攻的表現。 法樂仕也有4人得分達到雙位數，鮑松和馬索各得13分，卡尼亞達和拉內特兩名小個子則同獲12分，前者還有6籃板4抄截進帳。

 


In a game pitting the experience and size of the Zhuhai Enterprise team against youth and speed of the Pharex Bidang Generix team, the former emerged victorious with a score of 73 to 66. The China Zhuhai Enterprise Group team scored their first victory of the tournament.

After last night's loss to the host team, Malaysia, the Zhuhai squad started off with a different line-up from the night before. The Chinese started two younger players with point guard, Zhang Hui and power forward, Li Jin, replacing Zhang Ji Xing and Zhao Renbin. This allowed them to play at a much faster pace than the previous game.

Liu Hailong started the game red-hot, scoring 10 points in the first quarter, allowing the Chinese an 8 point cushion into the second quarter. The Chinese finished the half with a 7-point lead and the score at 36 to 29.

The Pharex Bidang Generix team mounted a ferocious comeback in the second half, cutting the Chinese lead to 1 just two minutes into the third quarter. However, the more experienced Zhuhai squad went on a 13-4 run and pulled away yet again. Von Harry Lanete hit two 3-pointers at the end of the third quarter to bring the Philippines to within 6 points.

The Pharex Bidang Generix squad refused to go down without a fight, and started pressing the Chinese with 5 minutes remaining in the game. This aggressive defense cost the Filipinos their main scoring threat, Ian Chris Mazo, who fouled out of the game.

The Filipino squad were undermanned tonight, with their starting point guard John Carl Melegrito out with an injury. With only 9 players left to play, they were unable to come back and ended the night with another loss.

The Zhuhai squad shot the ball much better than in the previous game, hitting 44% p of their shots from the field. Sun Zhongcheng also chipped in 14 points and 9 rebounds and Qu Shaohua finished with a double-double of 11 points and 11 rebounds.

The Filipinos had four players scoring in double figures with Conard Fritz Bauzon and Ian Chris Mazo scoring 13 points a piece. Conard Fritz Bauzon had an all-round performance with 12 points, 6 rebounds and 4 steals and Von Harry Lanete added 12 points.
